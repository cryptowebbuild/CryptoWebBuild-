Case Study — TokenLaunch

Goal

Deliver a fast, trustworthy landing page for a Solana token presale that converts visitors into whitelisted presale participants and provides clear token documentation for auditors and buyers.

Actions
  • Built static, CDN-hosted pages on Cloudflare Pages for global performance and low latency.
  • Implemented structured data (JSON-LD), descriptive meta tags and sitemaps for indexing and enhanced SERP appearance.
  • Integrated live price feed and WalletConnect links to increase transparency and buyer confidence.
  • Optimized images, lazy loading and critical CSS to reduce LCP and CLS.
Results

The site launched quickly and provided a clear presale funnel and documentation for the token. The client reported improved user trust and smoother presale registrations following the redesign. The technical setup (CDN, optimized assets, structured data) improved indexing and discoverability versus their previous page.

Case Study — MemeCoinSite

Goal

Create a high-converting, brand-forward landing page for a BNB Chain meme coin designed to attract early supporters and collect presale interest while remaining low-cost and fast.

Actions

Case Study — ShopFast

Goal

Deliver a fast, easy-to-manage e-commerce experience for a small store that converts visitors into customers and keeps operational complexity low.

Actions
  • Built product listing and detail pages with semantic markup and product schema for better search visibility.
  • Implemented a lightweight client-side cart with secure Stripe/PayPal checkout options.
  • Optimized images, lazy loading and preconnects to third-party checkout domains to speed up checkout flow.
  • Added sitemap and product structured data to help Google index product pages properly.
Results

The store provides a lean purchase flow with fast page loads and clear product presentation. Structured data and SEO optimizations help product pages appear more clearly in search results and assist organic discovery.

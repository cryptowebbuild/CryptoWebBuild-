# CryptoWebBuild Agency Content Strategy (2025-2026)

## 1. Target Audience & Market
**Primary Markets:** USA, UK, Canada, UAE (Dubai).
**Target Client:**
- **The "Degen" Founder:** Wants to launch a Meme Coin fast (Solana/Base). Needs speed, mobile optimization, and "hype" design.
- **The Startup CEO:** Building a serious DeFi protocol or Exchange. Needs security, audit-readiness, and professional branding.
- **The E-com Owner:** Wants to accept Crypto payments.

## 4. Internal Linking Strategy (The "Spiderweb")
- **Homepage** links to -> **Services**
- **Services** links to -> **Relevant Case Studies** & **Cost Guide Blog**
- **Blog Posts** must link back to -> **Services** (The "Money Page")
    - *Example:* In the "Meme Coin Guide", link the text "hire a professional developer" to `/contact`.

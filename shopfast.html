ShopFast — Lightweight E-commerce Store

Fast product pages, simple JS cart and optimized checkout for small businesses.

Challenge

Slow, bloated e-commerce caused abandonment and low organic visibility. The client wanted a fast, low-cost storefront with good search presence.

Solution

  • Static product pages with semantic product schema
  • Lightweight JS cart + Stripe/PayPal integration
  • Image optimization and lazy load for fast LCP
  • Sitemap and product structured data for indexing

Impact

  • Page loads under 1s
  • Checkout abandonment down 38%
  • Rich snippets appeared in Google search

Goal

Create a viral-ready landing page that captures presale interest, presents tokenomics simply, and supports aggressive social campaigns.

Approach

Key actions

  • Bold hero with mascot and clear CTA to increase brand recall
  • Countdown timer and urgency-driven CTAs for presale conversion

  • Mobile-first optimization and lightweight assets for fast loads

Outcome

  • Clear analytics for each marketing channel and CTA
